# THEORY — ARGUS-OS1

## Centriole Pedigree Hypothesis

The sequence of division orientations predicts centriole fate (retained vs eliminated).

### Pedigree Score (PCA)
5 quantitative metrics per centriole:
- Fraction of parallel divisions
- Mean 3D angle change
- Angle variance
- Orientation switches
- Cumulative angular path

### Primary Test
Within-type pedigree comparison. Sister-cell pairs (Sulston 1983).
N = 100 embryos. Power >80% for OR≥1.5.

### Model Organism
C. elegans — complete cell lineage (558 cells at hatching).
~88% of somatic cells eliminate centrioles during embryogenesis.
