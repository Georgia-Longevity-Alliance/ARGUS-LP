Good question. A few reasons:

1. **Upright geometry.** The objective comes down from above, which is what we need for water immersion later on — gravity holds the water column in place. Inverted scopes need pumps and seals.

2. **v6.1.5 is the current stable release.** The motorised stage works, the software stack is solid, and there is a decent community around it. Did not want to chase a moving target while building custom mods on top.

3. **Simple mechanical mods.** Most of what we are adding — micromanipulators, heated enclosure, filtered air — bolts onto the existing frame without redesigning the core XY stage or optics path. v6.1.5 is modular enough for that.
